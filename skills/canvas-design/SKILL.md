Two-step approach: first articulate a "design philosophy" (naming an art movement, e.g.
"Brutalist Joy" or "Chromatic Silence"), then express that philosophy visually through
form, space, color, and composition. Text is treated as a minimal visual accent rather
than the primary content. Focus is on static visual communication output as .png or .pdf.
